Description

This recipe provides a foolproof method for creating a versatile and authentic pizza dough at home. It focuses on achieving the perfect balance of a crunchy exterior and a soft, airy interior, making it a great foundation for any of your favorite toppings. What makes this dough stand out is its simplicity and the use of basic pantry staples like flour, yeast, and olive oil. By following a few key steps in the rising process, you ensure a flavorful crust that rivals those from a professional pizzeria.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose or bread flour (Type 500 or 00)
  • Lukewarm water
  • Dry yeast (or fresh yeast if preferred)
  • A pinch of sugar (to activate the yeast)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast: Mix the yeast and sugar into lukewarm water and let it sit for about 10 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  2. Combine ingredients: In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt, then create a well in the center to pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Knead the dough: Work the mixture until a dough forms, then knead it on a floured surface for about 5–10 minutes until it is smooth and elastic.
  4. First rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean cloth, and let it rise in a warm spot for about an hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Shape the pizza: Punch the dough down to release air, divide it if necessary, and stretch or roll it out to your desired thickness.
  6. Bake: Add your toppings and bake in a preheated oven at the highest temperature possible until the edges are golden and crispy.
