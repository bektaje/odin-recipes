Description

These chocolate brownies are the ultimate treat for any dessert lover. They feature a rich, fudgy center with a signature crinkle top that melts in your mouth, providing a deep cocoa flavor that isn't overly sweet.

  • Unsalted butter (melted)
  • Granulated sugar and brown sugar
  • Eggs (at room temperature)
  • Vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • Chocolate chips or chunks (optional)

Steps

  1. Prep the oven and pan: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease an 8-inch square baking pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Mix fats and sugars: In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter and both types of sugar until well combined.
  3. Add wet ingredients: Whisk in the eggs and vanilla extract until the mixture is smooth and slightly pale.
  4. Sift in dry ingredients: Gently fold in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt using a spatula. Do not overmix, or the brownies will become tough.
  5. Add chocolate: If using, fold in the chocolate chips or chunks for extra gooeyness.
  6. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top. Bake for 20–25 minutes. A toothpick inserted in the center should come out with a few moist crumbs.
  7. Cool: Let the brownies cool completely in the pan before cutting them into squares to ensure they set properly.
